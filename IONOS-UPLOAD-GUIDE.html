🚀 Aventus Website - IONOS Upload Guide

Follow these simple steps to publish your website

1 Log into IONOS

Go to ionos.com and log in to your account

2 Access File Manager

Find "Websites & Domains" or "File Manager" in your IONOS dashboard

Look for the option to manage your website files

3 Copy Your Website Files

Below are all 5 files you need. Click "Copy to Clipboard" for each file, then paste into a text editor and save with the exact filename shown.

IMPORTANT: You need to create a folder called legal in your IONOS file manager, then upload the 4 legal files into that folder.
📄 index.html (Upload to ROOT folder)



    
    
    Aventus Digital Media - Elevate Your Digital Presence

Now copy the 4 legal files below...

4 Upload to IONOS

  1. Save index.html to your computer
  2. In IONOS File Manager, upload index.html to the root folder
  3. Create a new folder called legal
  4. Upload the 4 legal files into the legal folder

5 Test Your Website

Visit your domain name (e.g., www.yourdomain.com) and your website will be live!
